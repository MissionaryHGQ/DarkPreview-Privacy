Privacy Policy

darkP is a macOS utility designed to assist users with visual impairments by providing instant black-and-white color inversion of images.

Information We Collect

darkP does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not access your contacts, location, camera, or other sensitive data
  • All image processing is performed locally; temporary files are automatically deleted after use
  • In-app purchases are handled exclusively through Apple StoreKit; we do not receive any payment information
